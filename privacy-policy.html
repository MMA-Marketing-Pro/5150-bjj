Privacy Policy.

How 5150 Martial Arts collects, uses, protects, and shares your information.

Effective Date: April 23, 2026 · Last Updated: April 23, 2026

5150 Martial Arts ("5150," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at 5150-bjj.pages.dev (the "Site") and provides martial arts instruction at 352 Route 59, Nyack, NY 10960 (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect about you, how we use and share it, and the choices you have. It is written to comply with U.S. privacy law, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Advertising Standards, and Google Ads Personalized Advertising Policies.

By using the Site, submitting a form, booking a class, or becoming a member, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information you give us

  • Contact information: first and last name, email address, phone number, and the program you're interested in, submitted through our trial-class lead form, booking flow, or contact form.
  • Membership information: billing address, emergency contact, parent/guardian details (for minors), date of birth, medical conditions relevant to training, and payment details (processed by our third-party payment processor — we do not store full card numbers).
  • Communications: messages you send to us by email, SMS, or through forms on the Site.

b. Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, links clicked, referring URL, session duration, and approximate location (city/region) derived from IP.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: small data files set by the Site and by third-party analytics and advertising partners to recognize your browser, remember preferences, measure performance, and serve relevant ads. See Section 6 for details and controls.
  • Advertising and analytics identifiers: including the Meta Pixel, Conversions API, and Google advertising/analytics tags, which may collect cookie IDs, device identifiers, hashed email/phone, and event data (such as page views, form submissions, and booking completions).

c. Information from third parties

We may receive information about you from our scheduling/CRM provider (Go High Level), payment processor, email provider (Resend), and advertising platforms (Meta, Google). For example, if you click one of our ads and fill out a lead form, we receive the information you submit plus limited campaign data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, schedule your free trial class, and onboard you as a member;
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site and our Services;
  • Send transactional messages (appointment confirmations, schedule changes, billing receipts);
  • Send marketing messages about classes, events, promotions, and related offers — with your consent where required, and always with an opt-out;
  • Measure and improve the performance of our advertising on Meta, Google, and other platforms, including by creating lookalike and custom audiences;
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

3. Legal Bases & Consent

Where U.S. state law requires it, we process your personal information based on your consent, our legitimate business interests (operating the Site and advertising our Services), and to perform a contract (membership). You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing 5150martialarts@gmail.com.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information in the traditional sense. We share information with:

  • Service providers who help us run our academy and Site — including Cloudflare (hosting), Go High Level (CRM/scheduling), our payment processor, and our email/SMS provider — under contracts that restrict their use of your data.
  • Advertising platforms (Meta, Google) for measurement, audience building, and optimization. Meta and Google may treat some of this sharing as a "sale" or "share" under California law; you can opt out via Section 9.
  • Legal, safety, and compliance recipients when required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of 5150, our members, or the public.
  • Successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to standard confidentiality terms.

5. Advertising Disclosures (Meta & Google)

We use Meta Pixel and the Meta Conversions API to measure the effectiveness of ads on Facebook and Instagram, to build custom audiences (including from our website visitors and customer lists), and to create lookalike audiences. Meta processes this data under the Meta Business Tools Terms. You can manage your ad preferences at facebook.com/adpreferences.

We use Google Ads, Google Analytics 4, and related Google products for measurement, remarketing, and personalized advertising consistent with the Google Ads Personalized Advertising Policy. We do not use these tools to collect or infer sensitive categories (such as health, religion, or sexual orientation). You can control Google personalized ads at adssettings.google.com and opt out of Analytics at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Ages: Our advertising is targeted to adults (18+) — parents and guardians for our Kids and Tiny Sharks programs, and prospective adult students. We do not knowingly target advertising to children under 13, and we do not run ads based on sensitive personal categories prohibited by Meta or Google.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies help the Site function and help us understand what's working. We use:

  • Strictly necessary cookies (Site operation, security).
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand Site usage in aggregate.
  • Advertising cookies (Meta Pixel, Google Ads) to measure conversions and personalize ads.

You can block cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not work correctly if you do.

7. SMS & Email Marketing

When you submit a lead form, you consent to receive transactional and marketing SMS and email from 5150 Martial Arts about your trial class, membership options, and school events. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to any SMS to opt out, or click "unsubscribe" in any marketing email. Your carrier is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

8. Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Contact-form inquiries that don't convert are typically retained for up to 24 months. We use commercially reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards to protect personal information, but no system is 100% secure.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate information;
  • Delete your information (subject to legal exceptions);
  • Opt out of targeted advertising, "sharing," or "sale" of your data;
  • Limit the use of sensitive personal information;
  • Appeal a denial of any of the above.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at 5150martialarts@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will verify your identity before acting and respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. You may also designate an authorized agent. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

10. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 online. When a parent or guardian enrolls a minor in a program, we collect only the information necessary to deliver safe instruction (name, age, parent contact, relevant medical info) and we do not sell or share that information for advertising. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information through the Site, email 5150martialarts@gmail.com and we will delete it.

11. Photo & Video Release

We occasionally take photos and videos during classes, events, and promotions for use on our Site, social media, and advertising. If you (or your child) would prefer not to appear in any marketing materials, email 5150martialarts@gmail.com at any time and we will remove identifiable content upon reasonable request.

12. International Visitors

The Site is operated from the United States. If you access the Site from outside the U.S., you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the U.S., which may have different data-protection laws than your jurisdiction.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date above and, if changes are material, notify you by email or prominent notice on the Site before they take effect.
