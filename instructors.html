Meet your
professors.

Three Gracie-lineage professors who built 5150 into the #1 academy in Rockland County — not by accident, but by showing up for every student, every class, every week.

Leadership

The coaches behind
the academy.

Every class at 5150 is led by a full professor — not a rotating lineup of part-time coaches. You'll see the same faces, the same methodology, and the same feedback from your first class through your black belt.

Prof. Scott O'Keeffe

Head Professor · Co-Owner

Prof. Scott O'Keeffe is one of the founding professors at 5150 Martial Arts. His teaching philosophy blends old-school Gracie fundamentals with a modern, problem-solving approach — meeting each student where they are, whether you've never trained a day in your life or you're prepping for competition.

Prof. Irene Shapiro

Professor · Co-Owner

Prof. Irene Shapiro is known for her technical precision, patience, and deep connection with every student who steps on her mat. Students credit her with turning 5150 into a true second family — not just a martial arts school.

Prof. Kristofer Hardin

Professor

Prof. Kristofer Hardin brings years of high-level grappling experience to the 5150 mats. He's the coach who'll push your technique to the next level while making sure every round stays sharp, safe, and productive.
