Striking, conditioning, and a cardio workout that doesn't feel like cardio. Ages 16+ · Beginners welcome.

About the program

What you'll actually get on the mat.

Our adult kickboxing program is a dynamic mix of striking techniques, conditioning drills, and partner work that gets you in the best shape of your life without ever touching a treadmill. Whether you want to shed pounds, build striking skills, or just punch the stress of the week into a pad — this is the class.

Why this program works

What you'll walk away with.

  • Burn 600–900 calories per class
  • Learn real striking mechanics — jab, cross, hook, kick
