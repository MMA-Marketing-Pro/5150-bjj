Common questions

Kickboxing questions, answered.

Do I have to spar or get hit?

No. Zero contact is required — classes are built around pads, bags, and partner drills, and you train at your own intensity. Striking skills without taking punches.

Is kickboxing good for weight loss?

It's one of the best workouts in the building — our classes burn 600–900 calories while you learn real technique. It's cardio that doesn't feel like cardio, which is why people actually stick with it.

I've never thrown a punch in my life. Is that a problem?

Not at all. We teach striking mechanics from scratch — stance, jab, cross, hook, kick — and layer in combinations as you improve. Total beginners are the norm, not the exception.

What gear do I need for my first class?

Just athletic clothes and a water bottle. Don't buy gloves or wraps before your free trial — we'll tell you exactly what you need if you decide to join.

Can I train kickboxing and Jiu-Jitsu together?

Absolutely — plenty of our members mix striking with grappling for a complete skill set. Pair kickboxing with Adults Gracie Jiu-Jitsu and you cover both ends of self-defense.
