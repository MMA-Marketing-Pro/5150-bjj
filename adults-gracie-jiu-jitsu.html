Common questions

Adult Jiu-Jitsu questions, answered.

Do I need experience or to be in shape to start?

No. Our fundamentals classes are built for day-one beginners — most people walk in with zero grappling experience and average fitness. You set your own pace, and the conditioning comes from the training itself.

What should I wear to my first class?

Comfortable athletic clothes — a t-shirt and shorts or leggings work great. You don't need a gi for your free trial, and we'll walk you through exactly what to get if you decide to join.

Is live rolling safe for someone brand new?

Yes. New students start with positional, controlled drilling and are matched with experienced, size-appropriate partners. You roll when you're ready — nobody gets thrown into hard sparring on day one.

How many days a week should a beginner train?

Two classes a week is the minimum for steady progress; three is the sweet spot most of our adults settle into. Check the weekly class schedule — we run morning, evening, and Saturday adult classes.

Do you lock people into contracts?

No. Membership is month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Your first class is free, and we go over pricing in person after your trial so you can pick the schedule that actually fits.
