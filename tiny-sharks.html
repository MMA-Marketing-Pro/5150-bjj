Tiny Sharks — Parent & Me

Their first martial arts class, with you on the mat. Ages 3–5 · Parent participates.

About the program

What you'll actually get on the mat.

Tiny Sharks is an interactive class built for our youngest students and their parents. It promotes physical, mental, and emotional development — helping children build self-control, coordination, balance, and social skills while having fun with Mom or Dad right alongside them.

Why this program works

What you'll walk away with.

  • Bond with your child through shared movement and play
  • Build coordination, balance, and body awareness early
  • Develop focus and listening skills in a structured environment
  • Gentle, age-appropriate intro to martial arts fundamentals
  • Meet other parents raising kids the same age
  • An easy on-ramp into the Kids program when they age up
